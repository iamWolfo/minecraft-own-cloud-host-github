# ngrok

### To invite friends
bash : chmod +x ngrok
bash : ./ngrok authtoken 'AUTH_TOKEN'
bash : ./ngrok tcp -region='eu' '25565'

### Here are some common region codes you can use:
us: United States
eu: Europe
ap: Asia-Pacific
au: Australia
sa: South America
jp: Japan
in: India
